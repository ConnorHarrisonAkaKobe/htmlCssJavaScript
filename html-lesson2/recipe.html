Grandma's Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

flour, sugar, fat (like butter), eggs, and salt. Common additions for flavor and texture are leavening agents (baking soda or baking powder) and vanilla extract, with other ingredients like chocolate chips or nuts

Instructions

Preheat the oven and prepare baking sheets: Preheat the oven to \(350^{\circ }\)F (\(175^{\circ }\)C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper or lightly grease them.Combine dry ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.Cream butter and sugars: In a large bowl, use an electric mixer to beat the softened butter and both sugars together until the mixture is light and fluffy.Add wet ingredients: Beat in the egg and then the vanilla extract until just combined.Combine wet and dry ingredients: Gradually add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing on low speed until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.Add chocolate chips: Stir in the chocolate chips or other add-ins.Drop and bake: Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, leaving some space between them.
